=== REEL TITLE ===
पहली मुलाकात, पहला प्यार!

=== THEME ===
First Meeting

=== SCENE BREAKDOWN ===

--- Scene 1 ---
Visual: A vibrant, sunny park playground. Green grass, colorful swings, and blooming flowers in the background. Anaya is sitting alone on a small park bench near a flower bed, gently plucking petals from a flower with a curious, slightly shy expression. Suddenly, Aarav enters the frame from the left, confidently walking with his little briefcase swinging by his side. He spots Anaya, his eyes widening slightly. He stops, curious, but tries to maintain his 'businessman' composure.
Camera: Wide shot opening, then slowly zooms in slightly on Anaya. As Aarav enters, the camera tracks him, then settles into a medium shot showing both toddlers with a clear distance between them.
Characters: Anaya is dainty and focused on the flower, initially unaware of Aarav. Aarav has a confident walk, then his expression shifts to curious and slightly intrigued as he sees Anaya. He subtly adjusts his mini-tie.
Mood: Curious, innocent, beginnings of something new.
Duration: 8 seconds

--- Scene 2 ---
Visual: Aarav, still standing a little distance away, tries to get Anaya's attention. He subtly clears his throat or adjusts his briefcase loudly. Anaya looks up from her flower, her eyes meeting Aarav's for a fleeting moment. She quickly looks away, a rosy blush appearing on her cheeks, and hides partially behind the park bench or the flower bush. Aarav, undeterred, takes a small, hesitant step forward, a sweet, hopeful expression on his face. He glances down at his briefcase, as if contemplating his next move.
Camera: Close-up on Anaya's shy face as she looks up, then quickly shifts to a close-up on Aarav's hopeful expression. Ends with a medium shot of Aarav taking a small step, Anaya peeking from behind the bench.
Characters: Anaya is very shy, peeks with wide, curious eyes, then quickly hides. Aarav is determined but gentle, a sweet, soft smile forming on his lips.
Mood: Shy anticipation, gentle curiosity.
Duration: 8 seconds

--- Scene 3 ---
Visual: Aarav decides to make his move. He gently opens his mini-briefcase and pulls out a tiny, bright red apple (or a small, colorful toy car – something appealing to a toddler). He walks a few more steps closer, now just a few feet from Anaya, and extends the apple towards her with a hopeful, charming smile. Anaya slowly peeks out from behind the bench, her eyes fixated on the apple, then on Aarav's sweet, encouraging face. After a moment of hesitation, a genuine, radiant smile breaks across her face, melting away her shyness. She giggles softly.
Camera: Medium shot of Aarav approaching and offering the apple. Zooms in to a tight close-up on Anaya's face as her shy expression transforms into a beautiful, genuine smile and soft giggles.
Characters: Aarav is confident yet gentle, offering the apple with a winning smile. Anaya is initially surprised, then her shy curiosity turns into pure joy and a heartwarming giggle.
Mood: Heartwarming, joyful, breakthrough.
Duration: 8 seconds

--- Scene 4 ---
Visual: Anaya, still smiling, extends her tiny hand towards Aarav, not for the apple, but reaching for his hand. Aarav's eyes light up with delight. He gently puts the apple back in his briefcase and then tenderly takes Anaya's outstretched hand in his. They stand for a moment, looking at each other, their tiny hands clasped. Then, Aarav, still holding her hand, turns and gently pulls her along towards the swings, both of them beaming with happy, innocent smiles. The camera follows them as they walk hand-in-hand towards the swings, their giggles echoing in the sunny park.
Camera: Close-up on their hands as Anaya reaches and Aarav takes it. Pulls back into a medium tracking shot as they turn and walk hand-in-hand towards the swings, showing their joyful faces.
Characters: Anaya is playful and trusting, initiating the hand-holding. Aarav is protective and beaming, leading her gently. Both are expressing pure, innocent happiness.
Mood: Pure joy, connection, new friendship.
Duration: 8 seconds
